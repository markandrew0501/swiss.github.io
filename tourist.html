Tourist Spots

in Switzerland

01

SURPRISING ZURICH
In the heart of Switzerland, at the foot of the Alps and surrounded by hills planted with vineyards, Zurich is a little gem. Its typical corbelled houses, well-kept churches and, above all, its lake give it an undeniable charm.

02

OPULENT GENEVA
Bordering on France and easily accessible, Geneva is the ideal place to spend your first weekend in Helvetia. Aside from its famous jewelers, luxury hotels and banks, you'll find charming 18th-century buildings, as well as Saint-Pierre Cathedral.

03

THE MATTERHORN
The Matterhorn is Switzerland's best-known mountain. Nicknamed "the mountain of mountains", it is one of the most photographed mountains in the world.

04

ZERMATT AND ITS SKI RESORT
On the Italian border with the Valais, Zermatt, with its high, rugged and majestic Alps, is a strong symbol of Switzerland. Its ski area is not only the highest in the Alps, but also the best developed in the world.

05

THE ALETSCH GLACIER, THE LARGEST IN THE ALPS
In the south of Switzerland, in the canton of Valais to be precise, lies the largest glacier in the Alps.

06
